Concept Validation

We explored the disaster relief and recovery space to find a worthy problem to solve.

Problem Statement

Natural disasters are becoming more severe, and the failure chain that occurs after a disaster is often just as damaging. We researched the disaster management and recovery field, and persistent issues in disaster resilience.

  • Explore

Research

We conducted a competitive analysis of existing disaster recovery technologies, and interviewed experts in disaster management.

  • Explore

Puerto Rico Case Study

Puerto Rico was devastated by Hurricane Maria, and the entire island lost power. We interviewed locals and codesigned solutions based on the lived experience of a post-disaster community.

  • Explore

Design

We followed a design sprint process to ideate solutions around the problem of communication, validate the technical feasibility of those solutions, and codesigned a framework with Eaton to evaluate designs.
